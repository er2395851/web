Welcome

to the temporary landing page of the Moreno Valley College Software Engineering Club. We are a group of dedicated and skillful computer science students with a passion for programming and enhancing our knowledge of software development. We meet twice a week to share our knowledge, and work on projects. We also volunteer at and participate in regional events. If you're interested in joining, or would like more information, please contact us using the form below.


About the Software Engineering Club

Founded in the Fall of 2014 by a group of Computer Science tutors, The goal of the Software Engineering Club is to expand knowledge in programming and showcase the college's computer science program.
